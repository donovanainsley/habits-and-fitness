About Us


At Habits and Fitness, we're on a mission to redefine your fitness journey. Our goal is to foster a community of individuals who are dedicated to forging healthy habits, pushing their boundaries and achieve their fitness aspirations.


We are not your typical fitness bootcamp. We are a tribe of dedicated trainers, health enthusiasts and goal-driven participants who believe that the path to a better you begins with transforming your habits
